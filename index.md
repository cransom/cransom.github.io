##  4x Juniper SRX210b (Dell branded)

From parting out my network lab. Before Dell had their own networking equipment, they relabeled other productions, including the Junipers. Despite the logo, they are run of the mill Juniper SRXs and are no different than any other Juniper labeled product.

Each has 2x GigE port and 6 10/100 ports. Each can be set up as a chassis cluster and is perfect for a low cost hardware setup for HA, routing and firewalling labs. Power cables included. Tested and verified all ports work.
